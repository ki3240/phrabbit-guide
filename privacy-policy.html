English

Phrabbit ("the app") is an audio loop-practice app for language learning. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data the app does and does not handle.

1. No account, no data collection

The app does not require any account or login, and the developer does not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to its own servers. There are no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs in the app.

2. Your audio stays on your device

Audio files you import and any A/B loops, bookmarks, transcripts, or wordbook entries you create are stored locally on your device. The developer does not receive or have access to them.

3. Speech-to-Text

Automatic speech-to-text is performed using Apple's built-in Speech framework. Depending on your device and iOS version, recognition may run on-device or be processed by Apple. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. The developer never collects or stores your audio or the resulting text.

4. Podcasts

If you use the podcast feature, the app connects directly to third-party podcast providers to fetch feeds and episodes. Those requests are subject to the respective providers' privacy policies. The developer does not operate these services and does not collect data from them.

5. Purchases

The optional one-time premium purchase is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not receive your payment details.

6. Data deletion

Because the developer collects no data, deleting the app removes all locally stored content from your device.

7. Children

The app is suitable for a general audience and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

8. Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy: chichipoposan@gmail.com
